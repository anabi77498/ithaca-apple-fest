About the Performers

The Harvest Festival is hosting talent across Ithaca from singers to fire stick performers and everything in between. These entertainers work hard to make your time at the Harvest festival worthwhile so please consider checking then out and supporting!

Performances are an integral part of what the Ithaca Apple Harvest represents and is an extravagant showcase of the talent within our community! We will be having performances throughout the second and third day of the Apple Harvest Festival. These performances will be scheduled hourly so make sure you schedule time to stop by!
